# ONLINE -ECOMMERCE- SALES -DASHBOARD (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)
## PROJECT OBJECTIVE :
an interactive sales dashboard designed to turn raw online sales data into clear, actionable business insights. Here’s a breakdown of what went into it:
 Data Engineering & Modeling: Connected and joined multiple tables, writing custom calculations to clean and manipulate the data into a seamless model. Advanced Interactivity: Implemented complex parameters, filters, and slicers so users can easily drill down into the specifics and explore the data on their own terms. Visual Storytelling: Leveraged a diverse toolkit of visualizations—from classic bar and trend charts to scatter plots and geospatial maps—to make complex KPIs highly intuitive.
